About The Development

A Mosaic of Living Excellence

D'Tessera Residences draws inspiration from mosaic masterpieces, where individual elements unite to create breathtaking harmony. This 49-storey architectural landmark represents excellence in creating homes that transcend ordinary living.

Strategically located in the vibrant Central Park Damansara township, D'Tessera offers 671 thoughtfully designed units ranging from 1,100 to 1,331 square feet. Each residence is crafted to provide the perfect balance between urban convenience and natural serenity.

With a stunning 10-acre park at your doorstep and premium facilities spanning from the ground level to the exclusive sky deck on Level 49, D'Tessera redefines contemporary luxury living in the heart of Petaling Jaya.
